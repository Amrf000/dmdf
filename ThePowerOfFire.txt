Run Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne to play the modification.
After the game has been started, go to "Singleplayer" - "Custom Campaigns" and play either the English or the German campaign.
For multiplayer games choose one of the maps in the folder "The Power of Fire".
The folder "en" contains the English maps.
The folder "de" contains the German maps.

To disable the modification, just uninstall it or remove the file "War3Mod.mpq" from the directory of Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne.
The uninstaller is in the same directory.
Uninstalling the modification won't remove your savegames.
